Purpose

A digital HAC is the new replacement for the standard HAC traditionally done on pen and paper, it makes it easier for us to keep track of our tasks and manage risks more effectively. The digital HAC draws its risks and control from the same risk register developed for the project in its beginning stages allowing for HACs to be keep record of the same adjustments made for each project. WARNING: Once you commit to Submit and Lock the HAC you will no longer be able to edit the HAC as it will await your manager's approval and validaiton to be unlocked

UBuild Website

Creating a Digital HAC

1.Open HSEQ tab, click Risk Assessments HAC
2.Click new. Add project details to the form fill out
3.Click save/start HAC

Completing HAC details

1.Fill out site setup and environmental conditions, for repeated information click the reload button to use the information presented in the previous HAC
2.Add Permits and Site Photos
3.Either add relevant personnel manually or click the reload button to use the same people previously entered
4.Click Next

Completing PPE & SWMS Details

1.Fill out the PPE and equipment checklist required for site
2.Select ALL applicable SWMS used on the site for the day
3.Click next

Completing HAC Table

1.Select Project Register Tab
2.Type relevant activity in search bar and select all activities applicable
3.Click add items
